Tans

Create puzzling pictures:

The tangram is an ancient Chinese puzzle made from a large square cut into seven pieces. Each individual piece or shape is called a tan. The tans can be arranged to make pictures. Try coming up with pictures of your own.
You'll need:
  • pattern
  • posterboard
  • scissors

We'll add it to our Tangram gallery.
