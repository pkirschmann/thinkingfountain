Indicator Art

This is a creative way to introduce acids and bases. -Chris

    Collect:

  • 237 mL (1 cup) grape juice
  • 118 mL (1/2 cup) vinegar
  • 237 mL (1 cup) of water
  • 30 mL (2 tbl) baking soda
  • paintbrush
  • 3 plastic cups
  • white construction paper

Paint the paper with grape juice and let it dry - do this several times.

Mix baking soda and water - paint a design on the paper.

What if you did this activity backwards? What else could work like the grape juice? Try painting with other liquids or solutions.
