Pop Bottle Whirligig


Watch it whirl.
 We visited an artist named Vollis Simpson and found this whirligig in his shop. His was made from old beer cans, we made ours out of plastic pop bottles. Fourth graders at Museum Magnet School made them with us.
-Karen, Mike and Natalie
