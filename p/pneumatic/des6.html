Our machine has boxes where people can ride. The wind blows the boxes and the boxes go in a circle. And we have little people in the boxes. Then we have a lot of colors around the ride that makes them dizzy. And there are plastic boxes so that people can ride in it. It goes around and around. There can only be six people on the ride.
