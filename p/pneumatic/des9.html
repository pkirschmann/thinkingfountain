French Fry Setup

We hope you'll send us a message to tell us what you think.

By: Diana, Salina, Andrea

Our machine starts with wind power. We used a fan. The wind blows a ball down a slide into a tunnel then a short slide. It then hits a bottle of potato fries, then the fries fall into a frying pan into oil. Then we put the fries into plates when it is done.

Troubleshooting

We had a lot of problems. First was that we couldn't make a long machine so we took out some parts like a bear, some books, a windmill, and we had to make the second slide shorter. We couldn't make the windmill work, it was hard work.

Reminds me of . . .

Our machine is kind of like the one in a movie "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory." Both machines have a ball, a slide, and a short tunnel.
