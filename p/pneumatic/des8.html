The Cannon Rocket Ship

We hope you'll send us a message to tell us what you think.

By: Jessica & Lanica

Our machine is a rocket ship and it blows out people and rocket ships. Our machine is made out of cardboard and tape and styrofoam. You have a special fan, a hair dryer and you have to hold it just right for it to blow out the people and the rocket ship.

Troubleshooting

The trouble was that we had to cut the cardboard. At first we were going to make something different but it didn't work at all. We had to tape it and it wouldn't go because the things got stuck to the tape and the air could just go out. We solved it by fixing the tape so that the air couldn't go out. So that's how we solved it.

Reminds me of . . .

It reminds us of a rocket ship that I've seen on TV and a horn or a telescope.
