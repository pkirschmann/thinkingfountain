The Twirling Bell Ringer

I hope you'll send me a message to tell me what you think.

By: Colette

My machine is orange, yellow, white and brown, and it has little red bells on it. There's paper, a spool, styrafoam, bells, metal, a straw, and wooden cards. When the fan blows the cards the bell hits the cards and it makes noise. My first machine didn't work because the cards didn't move. So I came up with this machine. I have never seen another machine like this one.

Troubleshooting

When the fan hits it it doesn't go right away.

Reminds me of . . .

A merry go round except for the horses that go up and down.
