Motion Picture

Try painting a picture with air.

You'll need:

  • paper
  • tempera paint
    (watered down)
  • a straw
  • strong set of lungs

1) Drop paint on the paper.

2) Use the straw to blow the paint.
SEND US YOUR PAINTING!
We'll add it to our Air-Blown-Art gallery.
What happens if you use more than one color? How far can you blow one drop? Challenge a friend to a race. Make another painting with the paper hanging on the wall.
