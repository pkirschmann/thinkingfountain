Termites 

Wood is one of the
least digestible
substances in the
forest, but
surprisingly it is
made of one of the
most sought-after
substances, sugar.
Termites are wood-
decomposing insects,
but they are able to
do their work only
with the assistance
of specialized micro-
organisms that live
in their digestive
tracts.
