Straws

Make a glider:

You'll need-

  • pattern
  • paper
  • straws
  • tape
  • scissors

Cut the strips (pattern)
Form strips into circles
Tape one circle to end of straw
Tape other circle to opposite end
It's ready to fly!

Try different shapes.
How high does each fly?
How far do they glide?
Try aiming for a target.
